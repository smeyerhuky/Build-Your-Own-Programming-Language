## Cost structure

### Cost of revenue (COGS)

| Cost item | Type | Estimated monthly (Year 1) |
|---|---|---|
| Cloud infrastructure (sandbox compute) | Variable | $1 500–$3 000 |
| LLM API costs (GPT-4o or Claude) | Variable | $2 000–$5 000 |
| CDN and storage | Variable | $200–$500 |
| Payment processing (Stripe, 2.9 % + $0.30) | Variable | ~3 % of revenue |

**Estimated gross margin (Year 1):** 65–72 %  
**Target gross margin (Year 3):** 78–82 % (as LLM costs decline and scale efficiencies kick in)

### Operating expenses

| Cost item | Type | Estimated monthly (Year 1, post-hire) |
|---|---|---|
| Salaries (Clinton + founding engineer + DevRel) | Fixed | $45 000 |
| Marketing (content, tools, conference) | Semi-fixed | $4 000 |
| Legal and compliance | Fixed | $1 500 |
| Software subscriptions (Stripe, Datadog, Sentry, etc.) | Fixed | $800 |
| Office / workspace | Fixed | $0 (remote-first) |

**Estimated monthly burn (Year 1):** ~$55 000–$65 000  
**Runway on $750K pre-seed:** ~12–13 months (target $10K MRR before runway exhausted)

## Key financial metrics

| Metric | Definition | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Year 3 Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| MRR | Monthly recurring revenue | $10K | $50K | $200K |
| ARR | MRR × 12 | $120K | $600K | $2.4M |
| Gross margin | (Revenue − COGS) / Revenue | 68 % | 74 % | 80 % |
| Monthly churn | % of subscribers who cancel/month | <5 % | <4 % | <3 % |
| NRR | Net revenue retention | 95 % | 105 % | 115 % |
| CAC (blended) | Fully-loaded cost to acquire one paying customer | <$30 | <$40 | <$50 |
| LTV (Pro) | Lifetime value of a Pro subscriber | $290 (10 mo) | $400 | $600 |
| LTV:CAC | LTV / CAC ratio | 9:1 | 10:1 | 12:1 |
| Payback period | Months to recover CAC | 1 mo | 1.5 mo | 2 mo |

## Break-even analysis

At the current cost structure (~$55K/month burn post-hire), LangCraft reaches **operating break-even at approximately $80K MRR**. Based on the model above, this occurs in **Year 3 Q1**.

Until then, the company operates on investor capital. The seed round ($2–3M, targeted Q4 2027) provides 24+ months of runway to reach break-even.
