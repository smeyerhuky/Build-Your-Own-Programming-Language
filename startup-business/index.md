# LangCraft Inc. — Startup Business Documentation

This bundle is the authoritative **business documentation** for **LangCraft Inc.**, the startup built on top of the *Build Your Own Programming Language* course, J0 language, and compiler toolchain. It follows the same Open Knowledge Format (OKF) conventions used in the [`/startup/`](../startup/index.md) technical onboarding bundle.
