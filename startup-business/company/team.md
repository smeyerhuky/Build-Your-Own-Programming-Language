## How we work

- **Remote-first** with an anchor in Socorro, NM and optional New York / San Francisco presence for investor meetings.
- **Async by default.** Decisions are documented in markdown (this repo). Meetings are for alignment and unblocking, not for doing the work.
- **OKF-everything.** Major decisions, specs, and plans are written up as OKF documents in this repo before being acted on.
- **Open compensation.** Compensation bands and equity targets are documented in `legal-structure.md` and will be shared with every candidate.

---

## Culture add (not culture fit)

We hire for skills, track record, and values alignment. We deliberately seek people who bring perspectives we don't already have. Compiler construction has historically been a narrow demographic — we intend to change that.
