# Overview

The chapter shows that once you have an AST you have a lot of
options besides codegen: pretty-print to a different language,
inline macros, mechanically refactor.

## Themes

- AST-to-AST rewriting.
- Source-to-source translation (TypeScript → JavaScript style).
- Preprocessor expansion (C-style macros) vs. hygienic macros
  (Scheme-style).
