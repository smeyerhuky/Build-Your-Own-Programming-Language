# Overview

Book chapter only; the repo ships no code. The chapter argues that
custom languages — especially DSLs — beat libraries when the domain
has its own algebra (queries, configs, contracts, simulations).

## Themes

- General-purpose vs. domain-specific.
- Greenfield vs. embedded DSL.
- The cost of *not* building one (config sprawl, ad-hoc parsers,
  growing libraries).
